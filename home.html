About Us

MISSION

We strive to offer our customers the lowest possible prices, the best available selection, and the utmost convenience of selecting their motorcycles at the comfort of their own home.














VISION

To be the #1 Best Selling Motorcycle company not just in the country but ALL across Asia, where customer can find and discover any motorcycle and services at the comfort of their own home.















HISTORY

The creators of the start-up company Skye Bikes intended to reach out to people who were having challenges purchasing a motorcycle. The organization is aware of how difficult it may be these days to purchase a motorcycle. Traveling back and forth between dealerships to compare costs and learn more about the motorcycle's features as well as its specifications can be difficult. After going through all of this, our CEO, Michael Cantalejo, along with his business partners, created a type of business where customers can view all the information they require on a single website rather than visiting a store and not knowing for sure if they will be able to purchase the item. At Skye Bikes, we offer the best services for motorcycles and also guarantee that we have the best team of mechanics that can examine and address all of your concerns.
